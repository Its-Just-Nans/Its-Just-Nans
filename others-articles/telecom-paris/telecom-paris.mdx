## 2022-06-01 - PACT - Autoform <Image src={pact} alt="pact logo" height={50}/>

PACT means "Projet d'Apprentissage Collaboratif Thématique" which translates to "Thematic Collaborative Learning Project".

A project consisting of a Desktop application in ElectronJS and a browser extension. Fill, save, and secure your information in the application, and use it via the extension to automatically fill forms.

Made with some classmates

Stack:
- ElectronJS
- NodeJS
- SvelteJS
- Jest
- Cypress
- GitlabCI
